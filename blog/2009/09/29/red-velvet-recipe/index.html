Red Velvet Cupcakes Recipe

Sep 29th, 2009

Quantity: This recipe makes ~22 cupcakes

Cake Ingredients

  • 2 c soymilk
  • 1 T apple cider vinegar
  • 2½ c flour
  • 2 c sugar
  • 1/3 c cocoa powder
  • 1 t baking powder
  • 1 t baking soda
  • ½ t salt
  • 2/3 c sunflower oil
  • 4 t vanilla extract
  • 1 c cooked blended beets
  • egg replacer equivalent to 2 eggs

Cake Directions

Mix together the soymilk and the vinegar and let stand.

Sift the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.

Add the sunflower oil, beet puree, and vanilla extract to the curdled soy milk, and mix.

Pour liquid ingredients into the dry ingredients, and mix.

Bake in a 350º oven, 20 minutes for cupcakes and minutes for cakes.
