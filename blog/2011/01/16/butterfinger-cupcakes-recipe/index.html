Butterfinger Cupcakes Recipe

Jan 16th, 2011

Quantity: 24 chocolate cupcakes w/ butterfinger icing

Batter Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ c unbleached white flour
  • 2/3 c cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ t baking soda
  • 1 t baking powder
  • ½ t salt
  • 1 ¾ c sugar
  • 2/3 c sunflower oil
  • 2 c soymilk
  • 1 T vanilla extract
  • 1 T apple cider vinegar
  • 8 chick-o-sticks (mashed into chunks)

Icing Ingredients:

  • ¾ c earth balance
  • ¾ c palm shortening
  • ¼ c water or soymilk
  • 1 T vanilla extract
  • 4 ½ c powdered sugar (sifted)
  • 4 chick-o-sticks (mashed into crumbs)

Cupcake Directions:

Preheat the oven to 350º.  Line 2 muffin pans with paper liners or set up 24 silicone wrappers on 2 baking sheets.

Combine vinegar and soymilk and let sit.  In another bowl, sift the flour, cocoa, soda, powder, and salt.

Add oil, sugar, and vanilla to the soymilk mixture and stir well.  Pour liquid into dry and mix until smooth. Add chunks of chick-o-sticks.

Fill the baking cups up to 2/3 full. Do not overfill.

Bake for 18-20 minutes, until when you insert a toothpick into the center of one, it comes out clean.

Icing Directions:

Using an electric mixer, whip up the first four icing ingredients, sifting in powdered sugar slowly and adding additional soymilk if it gets too thick. Add the chick-o-stick crumbs last, saving a few to sprinkle on top if interested. Pipe onto cooled cupcakes with a wide tip.  If you don’t have a piping bag, you can use a plastic bag with a hole cut in the corner.
