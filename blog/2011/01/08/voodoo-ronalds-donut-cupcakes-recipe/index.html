Voodoo Ronalds Donut Cupcakes Recipe

Jan 8th, 2011

Quantity: This recipe makes ~22 vanilla cupcakes w/ vanilla custard filling and chocolate icing

Ingredients:

  • 3 c unbleached white flour
  • 2 T cornstarch
  • 1 t baking soda
  • ½ T baking powder
  • ½ t salt
  • 1 ½ c sugar
  • 2/3 c sunflower oil
  • 1 ½ c soymilk
  • ½ water
  • 1 T vanilla extract
  • 1 T apple cider vinegar

Filling

  • 1 c soymilk
  • 1 t agar powder
  • 3 T arrowroot powder
  • 1 package silken tofu
  • 2/3 c powdered sugar
  • 1 T vanilla extract

Icing

  • 12 oz bag of chocolate chips
  • ½ c soymilk
  • 3 T agave nectar

Cake Directions:

Preheat the oven to 350º.  Line muffin pans with paper liners or set up silicone wrappers on baking sheets.

Combine vinegar and soymilk and let sit.  In another bowl, sift the flour, soda, powder, cornstarch, and salt.

Mix oil, sugar, water, vanilla, and the soymilk mixture and beat for 2 minutes.  Add dry ingredients slowly and mix until smooth.

Fill the baking cups about 2/3 full.

Bake for 18 minutes, until when you insert a toothpick into the center of one, it comes out clean.  Cool for 2 minutes in pan, then remove and put on a cooling rack.

Filling Directions:

Boil half the soymilk and the agar.  Once boiling, reduce heat and simmer for 3 minutes or until the agar dissolves.

Whisk half the soymilk and the arrowroot.  Add mixture to pot and whisk for 3 minutes over heat.  Remove from heat.

Blend tofu, sugar, and cooked mixture until creamy. Add vanilla extract and blend more. Transfer to tupperware with lid and chill for 2-3 hours.

Icing Directions:

Boil the soymilk and then remove from heat and add remaining ingredients. Mix until smooth.

Dip the top of each filled cupcake into the ganache and set on a cooling rack to harden.
