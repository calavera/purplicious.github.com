E.T. Cupcake Recipe

Jan 2nd, 2011

Quantity: This recipe makes ~22 chocolate cupcakes w/ p.b. icing (because 12 is never enough)

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ c unbleached white flour
  • 2/3 c cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ t baking soda
  • 1 t baking powder
  • ½ t salt
  • 1 ¾ c sugar
  • 2/3 c sunflower oil
  • 2 c soymilk
  • 1 T vanilla extract
  • 1 T apple cider vinegar

Icing

  • ¾ c earth balance
  • 2/3 c peanut butter
  • 3-4 T water or soymilk
  • 1 T vanilla extract
  • 1 t molasses
  • 3 c powdered sugar
  • 24 chocolate chips (optional decoration)

Cupcake Directions:

Preheat the oven to 350º.  Line 2 muffin pans with paper liners or set up 24 silicone wrappers on 2 baking sheets.

Combine vinegar and soymilk and let sit.  In another bowl, sift the flour, cocoa, soda, powder, and salt.

Add oil, sugar, and vanilla to the soymilk mixture and stir well.  Pour liquid into dry and mix until smooth.

Fill the baking cups about 2/3 full.

Bake for 18-20 minutes, until when you insert a toothpick into the center of one, it comes out clean.

Icing Directions:

Using an electric mixer, whip up all the icing ingredients, sifting in powdered sugar slowly and adding soymilk when it gets too thick.  Pipe onto cooled cupcakes.  If you don’t have a piping bag, you can use a plastic bag with a hole cut in the corner.  If you do have an icing bag or injector with a jelly donut fitting, you can fill the center of each cupcake with extra icing.
